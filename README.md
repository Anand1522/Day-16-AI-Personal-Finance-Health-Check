# AI Personal Finance Health Check Pro

## 🚀 Day 16 of my 30 Days 30 AI Websites Challenge

AI Personal Finance Health Check Pro V2 is a finance analysis web application that helps users evaluate their financial health using income, expenses, investments, savings, and emergency fund data.

The platform generates AI-powered insights, financial scores, wealth projections, risk assessments, and personalized recommendations to improve financial decision-making.

## ✨ Features

* Financial Health Score (0–100)
* Financial Personality Analysis
* Wealth Potential Score
* Financial Age Estimation
* Risk Assessment
* Emergency Fund Readiness Check
* Savings & Investment Analysis
* Future Wealth Projections
* Financial Stability Meter
* AI-Powered Insights & Recommendations
* Achievement Badge System
* Financial Report Generator
* Copy & Download Report Features
* Responsive Design

## 📋 How It Works

1. Enter your income and monthly expenses.
2. Add investment and emergency fund details.
3. Click **Analyze Finance**.
4. View your:

   * Financial Health Score
   * Wealth Potential
   * Financial Age
   * Risk Level
   * Future Wealth Projections
   * AI Recommendations

## 📌 Example

**Input**

* Income: ₹50,000
* Rent: ₹12,000
* Food: ₹6,000
* Investment: ₹8,000
* Emergency Fund: ₹1,20,000

**Output**

* Financial Health Score: 87/100
* Risk Level: Low
* Financial Personality: Growth Investor
* Wealth Potential: 90%
* Future Projection: Strong Long-Term Growth
